How it works

-- Step 1

Tell us what’s going on

We start with a free 15-minute call with a Brightline Care Navigator, so you can tell us more about your concerns and see how we can help.

-- Step 3

Make a plan forward

From there, we’ll build a care plan together, using evidence-based programs to reach your goals for your child’s care.

-- Step 5

We have your back

You’ll have a go-to Brightline Coach, care team check-ins, and progress updates —and after your child completes their program, we’ll continue to support you as needed.

-- Step 2

Meet your child’s therapist

From what you told us about your child’s needs, we’ll pair you with the right therapist. You’ll meet them first to make sure it’s a good fit!

-- Step 4

Get started on the right path

We’ll start your child’s program, including (in most cases) weekly sessions and equipping you with interactive exercises you can use in between.
